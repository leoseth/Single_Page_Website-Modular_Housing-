Welcome
to our industry


We love building projects with prefabricated modules. No chaos on construction, no trouble working in warm weather, rain or heavy storm: a complete house is being produced in a factory. Tempohousing, build with prefab modules! Key benefits: quality ( controlled environment, strict procedures), beneficial (the whole process is made much easier) and especially: speed. Our modules are somewhat bound to the standard permitted transport sizes. Thus we build between 2.4 and 3.5 meters wide and between 6 and 14.5 meters long. That fits well on a truck. And if we use a 2.4-meter ISO container, we can transport it very profitable, worldwide.

About Our Industry


History in Words

The company started in 2002 with the introduction of shipping container homes. We have built the largest student village in the world. Initially the project was a temporary solution for only 5 years and it is now in use for more than 10 years and will stay in use until at least 2018. While our early plans were to focus on temporary solutions, all projects have proven to be suitable for permanent use also!

We are famous for all the shipping container projects we have done in many countries, but today we also provide other modular systems from steel and light weight concrete, to serve a wider range of markets and customer.
