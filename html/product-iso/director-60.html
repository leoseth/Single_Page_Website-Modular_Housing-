Director 60

❮ ❯

This model is made up of two coupled 40ft sea containers.

These 2 containers 4.8 m (2 x 2.4m) wide and 12m long are connected to the long sides.

By combining the 2 containers, a floor area of approximately 60m2 is realized.

Comfortable, flexible and larger rooms are possible. This model is exceptionally suitable for (semi-) permanent housing, students, the starter market,

housing of staff or as (apart-) hotel rooms.

These units can be delivered worldwide.

Director 60 specifications

■Based on ISO sea containers, steel construction

■Can be stacked up to 7 layers (without auxiliary construction)

■Easy to transport by ship and truck

■Comes fully prefabricated at the construction site, easy to connect

■Equipped with: Kitchen, bathroom, E- and W installations, doors, windows, possibly upholstered and furnished to provide (different formats possible)

■Additional wall coverings possible (not required!)

■External components such as stairwells, balconies and galleries also provide Tempohousing
