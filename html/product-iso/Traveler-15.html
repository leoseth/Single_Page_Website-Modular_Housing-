Traveller 15

❮ ❯

This module is based on one 20 ft ISO shipping container. With a surface of about 15m2, this shipping container (2.4 wide and 6 meter long) is ideal

to convert to compact and comfortable homes. Everywhere transportable, sturdy, can be stacked high and it is no doubt the most sustainable way to

build homes. Suitable for staff accommodation, hotel rooms, urgency shelters. Worldwide availability.

Traveller15 specifications

■Based on ISO shipping containers, complete steel structure

■Can be stacked up to 7 layers

■Easy to transport by ship and truck and easy to relocate

■Arrives fully pre-assembled: a “plug-and-play” product

■Including, electricity, window, door, heating, vinyl flooring and ventilation. Bathroom and - kitchen are optional (different floor layouts possible)

■External facade possible, not necessary
